## Target Personas & Value Props

### Segment 1: Enterprise DevOps/Compliance Lead
- **Problem:** "Agents keep making breaking changes. I can't trace who approved it."
- **EGOS Value:** Pre-commit enforcement + provenance signatures
- **Win Metric:** 30% reduction in incident response time

### Segment 2: Open-Source Project Leader
- **Problem:** "How do I let AI help but keep my repo's identity?"
- **EGOS Value:** Portable governance via `.guarani/`, zero lock-in
- **Win Metric:** Community contribution consistency maintained

### Segment 3: Regulated Industry (Finance/Healthcare)
- **Problem:** "Agents must be auditable. We need proof of decisions."
- **EGOS Value:** Full audit trail + compliance-ready logs
- **Win Metric:** Pass regulatory audits with AI agents

### Segment 4: AI Product Builder (5+ Repos)
- **Problem:** "Rules keep drifting. Each repo does things differently."
- **EGOS Value:** One `.guarani/` via symlinks; change once, propagates everywhere
- **Win Metric:** 100% governance consistency across fleet
