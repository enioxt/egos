What is Gem Hunter?

Gem Hunter is an autonomous discovery engine that continuously scans GitHub, Hacker News, and developer forums to surface repositories you'd actually want to know about. It runs nightly, scores candidates across multiple dimensions — code quality, documentation completeness, community traction, and dependency freshness — and filters out noise before the results reach you.

The engine doesn't reward stars alone. A two-week-old repo with a clean API, working tests, and a real use case scores higher than a viral project with no LICENSE file. Each gem comes with a one-line reason and a quality score so you can decide in under ten seconds whether it deserves a deeper look. No sponsored placements, no affiliate links, no hot-takes. Just repositories earning their spot through the work.
