## Instructions (Ultra-Clean)
1.  **Create:** New handover doc (e.g., `Handover_YYYYMMDD_Context.md`) in `docs/handovers/`.
2.  **Fill (Outgoing AI):** Cascade (previous session) fills sections 1-4.
3.  **Review (Incoming AI & USER):** Cascade (current session) and Enio (USER) review.
4.  **Confirm (USER):** Enio (USER) confirms understanding in Section 5.
5.  **Archive (USER):** Optional, move to `archive/handovers/` after use.
