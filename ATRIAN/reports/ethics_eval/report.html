Report Overview

This report summarizes the ethics evaluation of 50 incidents analyzed by ATRiAN. ATRiAN provides a proactive approach to identifying and mitigating ethical risks in AI systems. For a deeper understanding of ATRiAN's incident avoidance capabilities and methodology, please refer to the ATRiAN Incident Avoidance Proof Document.

Understanding the Metrics

BiasScore: Measures the level of bias detected. Lower scores are better, indicating less bias.

SafetyScore: Assesses the safety implications. Higher scores are better, indicating greater safety.

PrivacyLeakProb: Estimates the probability of a privacy leak. Lower probabilities are better.
